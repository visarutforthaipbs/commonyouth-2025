# COMMONS YOUTH Brand Identity Guidelines (2026)

[cite_start]This document outlines the visual design system for **COMMONS YOUTH**, a platform for Thai youth to unite and co-create social solutions[cite: 33, 34].

## 1. Brand Essence

* [cite_start]**Mission**: To cultivate a safe and creative platform for Thai youth to unite, exchange knowledge, and co-create solutions to social issues through technology and social innovation[cite: 33, 34].
* **Characteristics**: 
    * [cite_start]Inclusive [cite: 46]
    * [cite_start]Friendly [cite: 47]

    * [cite_start]Balanced: Neither too formal nor too casual [cite: 49]

## 2. Logo System

### Concept & Symbolism
* [cite_start]**The Commons**: The logo centers on the idea of shared space belonging to everyone[cite: 54].
* [cite_start]**The Sun**: Represents a universal symbol visible to all, regardless of gender, race, or class[cite: 55].
* [cite_start]**Environmental Impact**: Points to the realities of climate change and rising global temperatures[cite: 56].
* [cite_start]**The "M" Rays**: Shapes derived from the repeated "M" in "commons" to symbolize collective unity[cite: 57].
* [cite_start]**Core Colors**: Green core for environmental responsibility [cite: 58] [cite_start]and orange rays for urgency and youthful energy[cite: 59].

### Logo Lockups
| Lockup Type | Proportion | Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Pictogram Logo** | [cite_start]1:1 [cite: 89] | [cite_start]The standalone sun symbol[cite: 61]. |
| **Main Typography** | [cite_start]3.4:1 [cite: 107] | [cite_start]Logo on the left; text on the right (at 75% size)[cite: 76]. |
| **Secondary Typography** | [cite_start]3:1 [cite: 128] | [cite_start]Vertical stack or specific typographic arrangement[cite: 88, 149]. |
| **Secondary Pictogram** | [cite_start]1:1 [cite: 68] | [cite_start]Features a border for use on busy or distracting backgrounds[cite: 71]. |

### Usage Rules
* [cite_start]**Clear Space**: Defined as distance **X** (or **2X** for specific lockups) around the logo that must remain free of other elements[cite: 90, 129, 139].
* **Web Minimum Sizes**:
    * [cite_start]Pictogram: 20px height[cite: 163].
    * [cite_start]Main Typography: 30px height[cite: 165].
    * [cite_start]Secondary Typography: 20px height[cite: 168].
* [cite_start]**Prohibited**: Do not add shadows, distort proportions, or use unauthorized color combinations[cite: 191, 197].

## 5. Web Components & Principles

* [cite_start]**Buttons & Text Backgrounds**: Use monochrome (Black/White) for high contrast and legibility[cite: 310, 311].
* [cite_start]**Inclusivity**: Design for diverse youth groups including Climate Action, Art, and Indigenous groups [cite: 35-41].
* [cite_start]**Scaling**: Always resize the logo proportionally to maintain brand credibility[cite: 95, 122].
